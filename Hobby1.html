Hobby 1 -- Music

Playing Instruments

      I enjoy a range of hobbies, but playing musical instruments is my favorite. I can play guitar, piano, ukulele, and kalimba. Whenever I have some free time, I like to cover songs and even write my own. Mixing different instruments together to create a nice accompaniment is something I really enjoy. It helps me unwind and release stress after a long day at work.

      Here are some songs that I covered. The first one, Song of the Lonely Mountain, is the theme song of movie The Hobbit, which is one of my favorite series of movie. I used guitar, piano, drump, and kalimba to make this song about 4 to 5 years ago.

      The second song is a Chinese song called Hua Hai, which means the Flower Sea in English. The singer is Jay Chou, who is one of the most popular singer in China. I covered this song recently, with piano and guitar mainly, plus a little bit of chorus.

Guitar

      Out of all these instruments, the guitar holds a special place in my heart. With the guitar, I have the freedom to sing along while playing, focus solely on the melody, or experiment with crafting songs using various chords. I embarked on my guitar journey independently around four years ago. Throughout these years, I've devoted countless hours to playing, almost every day, without ever feeling weary. Whenever I feel stressed or anxious due to school, the guitar serves as a reliable source of solace, effectively calming my mind.
