Hobby 2 -- Sport

Tennis

      I possess proficiency in various sports, with tennis being my favorite. My parents' fervor for tennis influenced me from a young age, leading me to begin playing when I was little. From the age of 10, I dedicated at least an hour daily to tennis practice. By the time I turned 14, I commenced traveling extensively for training and competitions. This particular photo was captured in Illkley, Scotland, in 2017, affording me the opportunity to experience playing on a grass court for the first time. To date, it remains my sole encounter with such a surface.

NMSU Women's Tennis Team

      Right now, I am a member of the NMSU Women's Tennis Team, which I find highly enjoyable. Our team dedicates three hours to practice every day from Monday to Saturday, and during the spring semester, we engage in numerous matches. Almost weekly, we embark on travels to compete against other schools.
