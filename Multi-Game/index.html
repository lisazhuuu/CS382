Connect 4

Welcome to Mingfang Zhu's Connect 4 game! In this two-player game, the goal is to create a line of four discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent does. Let's explore the rules together!

  • Players can choose their pieces' color ONCE by dragging it to the gray area, filling both areas if color is chosen.
  • Once the game start, players can't choose their pieces anymore.
  • If the game is decided, an animation showing who wins will show up on top of the screen, and players can click NEW GAME button to start over.
  • If the game is undecided when all the tiles are full, click NEW GAME to start over.
  • Who's turn for the next play will be showed on the bottom right of the screen.
